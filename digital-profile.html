Your Action Playbook

Specific Actions for Denver Event Manager

Concrete, measurable tasks to drive partner engagement and tier progression in your market.

Weekly Actions

  • Send 20 Cold Outreach Emails 10 to properties, 10 to vendors from target list
  • Follow Up on 15 Existing Leads Move stalled conversations forward
  • Schedule 3 Discovery Calls Convert interest to conversations
  • Update CRM Pipeline Log all activities, move stages, add notes

Event Day Actions

  • Arrive 1 Hour Early Verify setup, meet property staff, check vendor readiness
  • Document Everything Photos, videos, resident reactions for marketing
  • Collect Feedback Forms 5-question survey from residents who participated
  • Debrief with Both Partners 10-min call with property + vendor within 24hrs

Monthly Reviews

  • Partner Tier Assessments Review metrics, identify advancement candidates
  • At-Risk Partner Outreach Call any partner with declining scores
  • Revenue Report to Lux Markets GMV, events, new partners, satisfaction trends
  • Pipeline Health Check Ensure 3x coverage for next quarter's goals
