## Executive Summary

### Business Overview
Lux Markets is a B2B SaaS platform targeting emerging Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) brands in the Austin, Texas market. The company operates a dual-sided marketplace:

1. **For CPG Brands:** Data analytics platform + access to exclusive pop-up market events at luxury apartment communities
2. **For Luxury Apartment Communities:** Turnkey event management for resident engagement through curated shopping experiences

### Core Value Proposition
"All-in-one data analytics platform with exclusive markets designed to help emerging CPG brands grow and succeed."

### Primary Revenue Model
- Monthly SaaS subscription: $24.90/month
- Per-event fees (amount not disclosed on website)
- 30-day free trial offered

### 7. MARKET RULES (/market-rules)

#### Vendor Requirements

**Application & Admission**
- All invitations are discretionary
- No guaranteed acceptance or continuation

**Pre-Market Obligations**
- Signed agreement required
- Products must be grown/prepared within 150 miles (Gulf seafood excepted)
- No reselling unless value is added

**Arrival & Setup**
- Arrive 30 minutes before start
- Fully set up 10 minutes prior
- No early breakdown
- Prompt breakdown at close

**Weather Policy**
- Year-round, rain or shine
- No refunds for weather or poor sales
- 48-hour advance notice required for absences

#### Penalty Structure

| Violation | Fine |
|-----------|------|
| Late cancellation (< 48 hours) | $25 |
| No notice absence (1st offense) | $30 |
| No notice absence (2nd offense) | $60 |
| Late arrival | $20 |

**Escalation:**
- 3 fines → 1-month suspension
- 2nd suspension → 2 months
- 3rd suspension → Permanent expulsion

#### Booth Requirements

| Type | Specification |
|------|---------------|
| Indoor | Maximum 6-foot tables |
| Outdoor | 10'x10' space, straight-legged canopy, 30-lb weights per leg |

#### Vendor Categories

1. **Agricultural Producers:** Grow/raise within 150 miles; 50% sales from own production
2. **Value-Added:** Processed with local ingredients; proper permits required
3. **Prepared Food:** Ready-to-eat; Class B health permit; kitchen within 150 miles
4. **Artisan:** Local creations or materials with local products

#### Conduct Standards
- No discrimination/harassment (warning → 1-month → expulsion)
- Professional presentation required
- No disparaging Lux Markets
- No smoking/vaping within market boundaries
