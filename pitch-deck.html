03

Phase 2

Finding Partners

Strategic approaches for both property managers and vendors, tailored to their unique needs and motivations.

Property Identification

We target Class A and B+ luxury apartments with 150+ units, strong amenity packages, and engaged management teams.

  • Average rent $1,800+/month
  • Community spaces available
  • Active resident programming
  • Management company receptive

Vendor Selection

We seek premium local vendors with proven quality, professional operations, and growth ambitions.

  • 4.5+ star average ratings
  • Established 2+ years
  • Mobile/pop-up capability
  • B2B experience preferred

Outreach Strategy

Multi-touch approach combining digital outreach, in-person visits, and referral networks.

  • Personalized intro emails
  • LinkedIn connection building
  • Industry event networking
  • Partner referral incentives
