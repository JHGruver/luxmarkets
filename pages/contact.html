Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost for properties to host?

There is no cost to properties. Lux Markets handles all event expenses including setup, vendors, marketing materials, and beverages.

What's the typical response time?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. Property and vendor applications may take slightly longer for review.

Where do markets take place?

Currently, all Lux Markets events are held in the Austin, Texas metro area. We're exploring expansion to other markets.

How do I check on my application status?

Existing applicants can email us at hello@luxmarkets.org with their name and brand for a status update.
