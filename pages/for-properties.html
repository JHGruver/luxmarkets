Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does this cost?

Our partnership model is designed to be budget-neutral or even revenue-positive for properties. We handle costs through vendor fees, not property budgets. Schedule a call to discuss your specific situation.

How much time will my team need to invest?

Minimal. After an initial 30-minute discovery call, your ongoing involvement is typically 15-30 minutes per event for coordination. We handle everything else.

What if residents don't show up?

Our average attendance is 150+ residents per event. We provide marketing materials and promotion strategies that drive turnout. We've never had a poorly attended event.

Can you work with our existing event calendar?

Absolutely. We schedule around your property's existing programming and can complement events you're already planning.

What types of vendors participate?

Premium CPG brands: artisan food & beverage, skincare, wellness, home goods, and more. We curate vendors to match your resident demographics and property positioning.

How do I get corporate approval?

We've worked with major management companies and can provide a one-pager specifically designed for corporate approval conversations. We're happy to join a call with your leadership if helpful.
