Our Story

From Idea to Impact

Lux Markets was born from a simple observation: emerging CPG brands struggle to reach their ideal customers, while luxury apartment communities want unique, memorable experiences for their residents.

Founded in Austin, Texas, we created a platform that solves both challenges elegantly. Our curated pop-up markets bring handpicked brands directly into the amenity spaces of premier residential communities, creating intimate shopping experiences that benefit everyone involved.

What started as a single market at a local apartment complex has grown into a thriving network of partner properties and vendor brands across Austin, with expansion plans on the horizon.

Our Foundation

Mission & Values

"To create meaningful connections between emerging brands and discerning consumers through curated, community-focused experiences that elevate both."

Curation Over Quantity

Quality is our north star.

We believe in thoughtfully selecting every brand that participates in our markets. This ensures residents discover products they'll genuinely love, and vendors connect with customers who become loyal advocates.

Community First

Building genuine connections.

Every decision we make considers the impact on the communities we serve. From the brands we select to the experiences we create, we prioritize what's best for residents and the properties that host us.

Effortless Excellence

Professional, turnkey execution.

We handle every detail so our partners don't have to. From vendor coordination to event setup to post-market cleanup, we deliver a seamless experience that looks and feels premium at every touchpoint.

Growth Partnership

Success is shared.

We're invested in the success of every brand and property we work with. When our vendors thrive and residents love their experience, we've done our job. We measure our success by the success of our partners.

Our Home

Why Austin?

Austin isn't just where we're based - it's the perfect incubator for what we do. The city's unique blend of entrepreneurial energy, appreciation for local businesses, and booming luxury residential market creates the ideal environment for Lux Markets.

Austin residents embrace discovery and support emerging brands. Property managers here are innovative and eager to provide unique amenities. And the city's thriving CPG scene gives us access to exceptional vendors who are ready to connect with their ideal customers.

As we grow, we carry Austin's spirit of authenticity and community with us.
