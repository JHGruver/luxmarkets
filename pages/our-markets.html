The Experience

A Curated Marketplace, Elevated

Lux Markets transforms luxury apartment amenity spaces into vibrant, upscale shopping experiences. Our pop-up markets feature handpicked CPG brands, complimentary beverages, and an atmosphere of refined discovery.

Intimate, Exclusive Events

Each Lux Market is designed to feel like an exclusive shopping experience, not a crowded trade show. We limit vendor participation to ensure quality over quantity, creating space for meaningful brand-to-consumer interactions.

Market Formats

Events Tailored to Your Community

We offer flexible market formats to match the unique character and preferences of each property.

Daytime Markets

Weekend afternoons, perfect for families and casual shoppers.

Ideal For:

Properties with families, remote workers, and residents who enjoy leisurely weekend activities. Typically 12pm-4pm with brunch-style refreshments.

Evening Sip & Shops

After-work events with wine, cocktails, and curated shopping.

Ideal For:

Young professional communities, urban high-rises, and properties seeking a more social atmosphere. Typically 5pm-8pm with wine and craft cocktails.

Seasonal Markets

Holiday-themed events that create memorable experiences.

Ideal For:

Valentine's Day, Mother's Day, Fall Harvest, Holiday Gift Markets. Seasonal themes drive higher engagement and create annual traditions residents look forward to.

Private Events

Exclusive brand launches and VIP shopping experiences.

Ideal For:

New property openings, lease-up celebrations, or exclusive brand partnerships. Fully customized to create unforgettable resident experiences.
