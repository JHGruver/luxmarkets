Market Rules

Application & Admission

All invitations to sell at any market are at the discretion of Lux Markets. Acceptance isn't guaranteed, and existing vendors can be removed without guarantee of continued space.

Pre-Market Obligations

Vendors must acknowledge market rules via a signed agreement. All items must be grown/prepared within 150 miles of the market location (Gulf seafood is excepted). Reselling is prohibited unless vendors blend or infuse products to create their own offerings.

Arrival & Setup

Vendors must arrive 30 minutes before market start and be fully set up 10 minutes prior. No early breakdown is permitted, even if sold out. Prompt breakdown occurs at market close.

Weather & Holidays

Markets operate year-round, rain or shine. No refunds or fee discounts apply for weather or poor sales. Vendors must submit absence notices at least 48 hours in advance for non-holiday closures.

Violations & Penalties

Fine Schedule:

  • Late cancellation (under 48 hours): $25
  • No notice absence: $30 first offense, $60 second offense
  • Late arrival: $20

After three fines, vendors face one-month suspension. A second suspension extends to two months. A third suspension results in permanent expulsion.

Booth Requirements

Indoor:

Maximum 6-foot tables

Outdoor:

10'x10' spaces with straight-legged canopies requiring 30-pound weights per leg

Smoking/vaping is prohibited within market boundaries.

Vendor Categories

Agricultural Producers

Must grow/raise products within 150 miles; maintain 50% of sales from own production

Value-Added

Processed products using local ingredients when available; requires proper permits

Prepared Food

Ready-to-eat items under Class B health permits; kitchen within 150 miles

Artisan

Local creations or materials integrated with local products

Conduct Standards

Discrimination and harassment violate policies with escalating penalties: written warning, one-month suspension, then expulsion. Vendors must maintain professional presentation, display sanitary products, and avoid disparaging Lux Markets.
