## Executive Summary

Denver offers an exceptional landscape for Lux Markets expansion, with over 5,149 luxury apartments currently available and a thriving high-rise condo market. The city's affluent neighborhoods—Cherry Creek, LoDo, Downtown, and RiNo—feature properties with extensive amenity spaces ideal for curated vendor markets.

Key property management companies already partnered with Lux Markets in Austin (AMLI, Greystar) have significant Denver presence, providing warm introduction opportunities.

## Neighborhood Analysis

### Cherry Creek (Primary Target)

**Demographics:**
- Highest concentration of affluent residents
- Tree-lined streets, boutiques, cafes
- Community events: Cherry Creek Arts Festival, Summer Concert Series, Holiday Market

**Why Ideal:**
- Residents accustomed to local markets/events
- Strong "shop local" culture
- Premium retail environment sets expectations

**Target Properties:** Seasons, Laurel, NorthCreek, 250 Columbine

### LoDo/Downtown (Primary Target)

**Demographics:**
- Young professionals, urban dwellers
- High foot traffic
- Entertainment district proximity

**Why Ideal:**
- Dense population in small area
- Evening/weekend availability
- Social atmosphere

**Target Properties:** The Coloradan, Confluence, Quincy, Spire

### RiNo Arts District (Secondary Target)

**Demographics:**
- Creative professionals
- Craft/artisan appreciation
- Emerging luxury market

**Why Ideal:**
- Aligns with artisan vendor positioning
- Growing wealth in area
- New developments opening

### Golden Triangle (Secondary Target)

**Demographics:**
- Museum/cultural district residents
- Art collectors, professionals
- Urban sophisticates

**Why Ideal:**
- Cultural appreciation
- Acoma and other new high-rises
- Walkable to downtown
