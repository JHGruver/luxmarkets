**Prepared for:** Jacob Gruver's Wife

## Executive Summary

Denver's nail salon industry offers strong opportunities for skilled nail technicians, with average hourly wages ranging from $19-$30+ depending on salon tier and experience. The Cherry Creek area features the highest concentration of luxury salons, while downtown and emerging neighborhoods offer diverse options.

**Key Finding:** Luxury salons in Cherry Creek and upscale neighborhoods offer the highest earning potential, with top technicians earning $40,000+ annually plus tips.

### Compensation Structure

**Typical Pay Models:**
- **Commission:** 40-60% of service revenue
- **Hourly + Tips:** Base hourly rate plus gratuities
- **Booth Rental:** Pay flat fee, keep all earnings

**Beau Visage Example:**
- Average: $17-21/hour + tips

- Staff retention: Many 5+ year employees

## What to Look for in an Employer

### Green Flags
- Clear commission/pay structure
- Ongoing education opportunities
- Quality product lines
- Good online reviews (as employer)
- Clean, well-maintained facility
- Supportive management
- Flexible scheduling

### Red Flags
- Unclear compensation
- High staff turnover
- Outdated equipment
- Poor sanitation
- No continuing education
- Negative employer reviews

---

## Interview Questions to Ask

1. What is your compensation structure (commission %, hourly, booth rental)?
2. What products and brands do you use?
3. Do you offer continuing education or training?
4. What is the average ticket price for services?
5. How are walk-ins vs. appointments handled?
6. What are your busiest days/times?
7. Do you offer benefits (health, PTO, etc.)?
8. What is the client retention rate?
9. Can I build my own clientele?
10. What is your social media/marketing approach?

## Licensing Requirements

### Colorado Nail Technician License

**Requirements:**
- Complete approved training program (600 hours typically)
- Pass state examination
- Apply through DORA (Department of Regulatory Agencies)

**If Licensed in Another State:**
- Colorado offers reciprocity for some states
- May need to pass Colorado-specific exam
- Contact DORA for specific requirements

**Website:** [DORA Colorado](https://dora.colorado.gov)

## Earning Potential Maximization

### Tips for Higher Income

1. **Specialize in nail art** - Command premium pricing
2. **Build social media presence** - Attract clients directly
3. **Work in Cherry Creek/luxury salons** - Higher-spending clientele
4. **Develop repeat client base** - Consistent booking
5. **Offer premium services** - Gel extensions, intricate designs
6. **Upsell add-ons** - Treatments, art, repairs
7. **Maintain availability** - Peak hours (evenings, weekends)

### Income Scenarios

| Level | Hourly + Tips | Annual Estimate |
|-------|---------------|-----------------|
| Entry | $18-22/hr | $35,000-45,000 |
| Experienced | $25-30/hr | $50,000-60,000 |
| Specialist/Artist | $30-40/hr | $60,000-80,000+ |
| Top Performers (luxury) | $40+/hr | $80,000+ |
