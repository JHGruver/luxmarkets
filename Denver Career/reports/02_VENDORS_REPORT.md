# Denver Vendors Report
## Established CPG Brands & Artisan Vendors for Lux Markets

**Prepared for:** Jacob Gruver - Lux Markets Denver Expansion
**Date:** December 2024
**Purpose:** Identifying quality vendors who align with Lux Markets' curation standards

---

## Executive Summary

Colorado boasts a thriving food, beverage, and artisan goods ecosystem. The state's culture of supporting local businesses, combined with Denver's affluent consumer base, creates an ideal environment for recruiting established vendors focused on brand growth over pure sales volume.

Key categories identified: Craft Food & Beverage, Wellness & Beauty, Home Goods & Artisan Products.

---

## Vendor Vetting Criteria (Lux Markets Standards)

**Must Meet:**
- Established business (1+ years operating)
- Professional presentation and branding
- Product quality aligns with luxury positioning
- Focus on growth/brand building, not just transactions
- Reliable inventory and fulfillment capability
- Insurance and licensing current

**Preferred:**
- Colorado-based or strong local presence
- Wholesale/retail experience
- Previous pop-up/market experience
- Strong social media presence
- Unique/differentiated product

## Vendor Outreach Strategy

### Phase 1: Market Attendance (Weeks 1-4)
1. Attend Heart to Home, Denver BAZAAR, Colorado Markets
2. Scout vendors who meet Lux criteria
3. Collect business cards, note quality observations
4. Identify top 20 prospects

### Phase 2: Direct Recruitment (Weeks 5-8)
1. Reach out to scouted vendors with Lux Markets pitch
2. Emphasize brand growth vs. sales volume
3. Highlight exclusive venue access
4. Offer onboarding support

### Phase 3: Retail Reverse-Engineering (Weeks 9-12)
1. Visit Whole Foods, specialty stores
2. Identify local brands with retail presence
3. Research via Instagram/website
4. Cold outreach with credibility pitch

## Competitive Intelligence

### How Other Markets Recruit

| Market | Approach | Our Advantage |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| Farmers markets | Open applications | We curate/vet |
| Pop-ups | First-come | Invite-only, exclusive |
| Craft fairs | Booth fees only | Partnership model |

### Vendor Value Proposition

**For Vendors, Lux Markets Offers:**
- Access to high-income customers in private settings
- Less competition (8-12 vendors vs. 50+ at public markets)
- Weather-protected indoor venues
- Professional event management
- Data and feedback from residents
- Brand elevation through association
- Consistent scheduling and reliability
